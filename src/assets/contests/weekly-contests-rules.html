Weekly Bio Ball contests — full rules (Phase 4)

This page summarizes the in-product policy for version 1 contest rules. Phase 4 uses dry-run payouts only — no real-money entry, prizes, or withdrawals.

Eligibility & limits

  • Contests apply to Bio Ball only. Other modes do not count.
  • You may be entered in at most one open contest at a time for this game type (enforced when you join).
  • Your slate is the first N qualifying completed games after you join, in server time order, with timestamps in the play window [windowStart, windowEnd). N is the contest’s leagueGamesN (often 10).

Scoring window

  • The window is half-open: the end instant is excluded.
  • Only events on or after your joinedAt time count — there is no retroactive credit for games before you joined.

Tie-breaking

Among entrants with a full slate (N games): higher contest wins win; then fewer losses in the slate; then fewer abandons; then stable uid order. Partial slates rank below every full slate, then by wins, games played, and uid.

Prizes & cancellations

Payout lines are simulated for testing. If a contest is cancelled or voided, there may be no standings or payout — follow operator communications.
